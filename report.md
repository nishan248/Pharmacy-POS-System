# Retail Pharmacy Reports Guide

# Introduction

Retail pharmacy reports are essential for monitoring medicine availability, controlling stock, ensuring regulatory compliance, improving customer service, and maintaining profitability. Proper reporting helps pharmacists make informed decisions, prevent stock shortages, reduce losses from expired products, and improve patient care.

# Recommended Retail Pharmacy KPIs

* Daily sales value
* Number of prescriptions dispensed
* Average prescription value
* Gross profit margin
* Stock turnover rate
* Expired stock percentage
* Stock-out rate
* Customer satisfaction rate
* Inventory accuracy percentage
* Supplier delivery performance

Monitoring these KPIs regularly helps improve profitability, inventory control, customer satisfaction, and regulatory compliance.
